About Us

Glow Life Foundation, is a non government / non profit / non political organization in the capital of India which is working for the welfare of society.

  • Fully active and directly benefiting senior citizens and children
  • Encouraging families for education, healthcare, women empowerment & community development
  • Glow Life Foundation's aim is to impart all round learning.

“If we are to teach real peace in this world, and if we are to carry on a real war against war, we shall have to begin with children” We consider youth as the solution not as the problem because they have the potential to promote a culture of peace. Our ultimate goal is to attain happiness and peace. Our objective is to move towards the higher level of fearlessness and creativity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) section. Here are some common questions and their answers to help provide you with the information you need.

  • What is the Glow Life Foundation?

    The Glow Life Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged children and youth. It focuses on providing education, healthcare, and life skills development to empower them to lead better lives.

  • What is the mission of the Glow Life Foundation?

    The mission of the Glow Life Foundation is to create opportunities and transform the lives of underprivileged children and youth through education, healthcare, and holistic development, enabling them to reach their full potential and become positive contributors to society.

  • What programs does the Glow Life Foundation offer?

    The Glow Life Foundation offers various programs to support underprivileged children and youth. This may include educational initiatives such as scholarships, after-school programs, and vocational training. They may also provide healthcare services, nutrition programs, and counseling. Additionally, they may focus on life skills development, mentorship, and career guidance.

  • How can I get involved with the Glow Life Foundation?

    There are several ways to get involved with the Glow Life Foundation. You can volunteer your time and skills by assisting with their programs or events. You can also donate funds or resources to support their initiatives. Additionally, you can help spread awareness about their work through social media or by organizing fundraising activities.
